Preparation of datasets before passing to the ML classification models 1. Drop column(s), if required 2. Split dataset into train and test datasets in desired ratio 3. Create ‘n’ such splits for repetitive testing 3. Sample train and test datasets to have approximately 1:1 ratio of positive and negative samples 4. Standardize the dataset
